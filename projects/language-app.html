Fluently

Language Learning App in the making

Project Overiew

Challenge

Many language learning apps focus on teaching its users basic words and phrases, making it difficult for intermediate language learners to learn relevant vocabulary based on their interest.
Fluently is an app that will help intermediate learners improve their language vocab in the field/topics of their interest

Objectives

Working with a team of three, we had to research, design and test a mobile app that has a minimalistic and pleasant user interface and is easy to use
